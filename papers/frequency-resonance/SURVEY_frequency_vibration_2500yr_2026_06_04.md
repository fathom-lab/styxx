## TIER 1 — SETTLED: vibration is a universal *descriptive language* and a *real mechanism*

**The math is bedrock.** Fourier (*Théorie analytique de la chaleur*, 1822) proved any function can be
written as a sum of sinusoids — frequency decomposition is the literal mathematical basis of
"everything is a sum of vibrations." (Verified 3-0.) *Honest correction:* the popular story that Fourier
"came from heat, not waves" is **false** (refuted 0-3) — the trigonometric-series problem has deep roots
in the 18th-century **vibrating-string controversy** (d'Alembert, Euler, Daniel Bernoulli). So Fourier
is *continuous* with the Pythagorean/musical lineage, not a break from it.

**Classical wave physics makes vibration visible and lawful.** Chladni (*Entdeckungen über die Theorie
des Klanges*, 1787 — the founding work of acoustics): sand on a bowed plate jumps off antinodes onto
stationary **nodal lines** (Klangfiguren); pitch rises with nodal subdivisions, f ∝ (m+2n)² ("Chladni's
law," Rossing, *Am. J. Phys.* 1982). (Nodal figures 3-0; pitch law 2-1.) Helmholtz (*On the Sensations
of Tone*, 1863) grounded the place/resonance theory of hearing — the cochlea as a bank of tuned
resonators.

**The deepest physics is literally true.** Quantum **Field** Theory — experimentally the most precisely
confirmed framework in science — describes particles as *quantized excitations of underlying fields*.
"Everything is vibration" is, at the level of QFT, **not a metaphor**: an electron is a quantum of the
electron field. (Standard demarcation; medium confidence as framing — not among the re-verified atomic
claims.) **String theory** goes further (particles = vibrational modes of strings) but is **unconfirmed**
— elegant, not established. Keep these strictly apart.

**Brain rhythms are real, mechanistic phenomena** (not woo). Gamma (~30–90 Hz) creates brief (~3 ms)
excitability windows so input arriving at the right *phase* drives postsynaptic cells — neuronal
communication is **phase-dependent** (Fries, "Rhythms for Cognition," *Neuron* 2015; confirmed by
optogenetics, Cardin/Sohal 2009 *Nature*). (3-0.) Theta–gamma **phase-amplitude coupling** codes
*ordered* sequences — different items in different gamma subcycles nested in one theta cycle — the
basis of the nested-oscillation account of working-memory capacity / 7±2 (Lisman & Jensen, "The
Theta-Gamma Neural Code," *Neuron* 2013). (3-0.) **This is the real scientific core the operator's
frequency intuition is reaching for.**

## TIER 2 — LIVE BUT CONTESTED: is rhythm the *mechanism that routes cognition*?

This is where mainstream neuroscience genuinely doesn't know yet.

**Communication-through-Coherence (CTC) / binding-by-synchrony** (von der Malsburg; Singer; Fries):
rhythmic synchronization between neuronal groups *gates* whether one influences another. A named,
mainstream, actively-researched hypothesis — **not established fact** (3-0 as contested). The
contestation is documented from both sides:
- "Definitive causal evidence is still lacking," and CTC may have "the causal order wrong" (Greenwood &
  Ward, *PLoS Comput Biol* 2024).
- Coherence may be a **consequence** of communication, not its cause (Schneider et al., *Neuron* 2021).
- Gamma may be a *local* marker, not a long-range mechanism (Ray & Maunsell, *Nat Neurosci* 2015).

**Coherence can mislead.** Two regions with **no direct connection** can show high phase coherence purely
from common input — so coherence is *necessary-at-most*, never *sufficient*, for communication (Quax/
Tiesinga; the rationale behind imaginary-coherence / PLI methods). (3-0.)

**The causal-stimulation evidence (tACS/tES) is contested and arguably overhyped** (3-0): effects are
unreliable and highly variable, ~0 after publication-bias correction (g≈0.076, n=1386; Egger P=0.001),
and confounded by **peripheral** mechanisms — transcutaneous nerve stimulation (Asamoah et al., *Nat
Commun* 2019) and **retinal** phosphenes (Kar & Krekelberg, *J Neurophysiol* 2012) — not clean cortical
entrainment. *Counter:* direct entrainment also occurs (Krause 2019), and a 2025 macaque V2→V4 study
adds newer causal evidence — so this disputes *dominance*, not *existence*.

**Refuted overclaims (kept for honesty):** "attention routes by gamma frequency" (split 1-2); the
Gestalt "millisecond synchrony binds features" claim (refuted 0-3).

## TIER 3 — METAPHYSICS / FRINGE: is consciousness *made of* vibration?

Coherently stated, mostly **unfalsifiable**, disputed on whether true at all. The demarcation line.

- **General Resonance Theory** (Hunt & Schooler, *Front. Hum. Neurosci.* 2019): macro-consciousness
  arises from *shared resonance* via a phase transition in information exchange — but explicitly rests on
  **panpsychism** ("consciousness is always present, even in the simplest processes... complexifies as
  matter complexifies"). Critics attack whether it's *falsifiable at all* (arXiv 2004.03541). (3-0 on
  what it asserts; the unfalsifiability *is* the flag.)
- **CEMI** (McFadden, *Neurosci. of Consciousness* 2020): consciousness physically **is** the brain's EM
  field. Notably yields a **testable discriminator** — consciousness should track **synchrony**, not
  firing **rate**. The most concrete prediction the fringe offers. (3-0.)
- **Pockett** (2012): conscious experiences are *identical* with specific 3-D EM field patterns (LFPs);
  an identity theory; she holds the field is epiphenomenal (no causal power). (3-0.)
- **Orch-OR** (Penrose–Hameroff): microtubule quantum computation + gamma. The most-attacked theory —
  but, notably, the physics-based *refutations* from McKemmish et al. (*Phys Rev E* 2009) **did not
  cleanly win** our adversarial vote (1-2, 0-3-against). That means: *we cannot assert Orch-OR is
  debunked from the surviving evidence* — only that mainstream consensus (decoherence-time objections,
  Tegmark 2000) remains deeply skeptical. Honest: skeptical, not closed.
- **Popular "40 Hz" / "raise your vibration":** gamma-entrainment health claims have *some* live science
  (MIT 2025 mouse work) but the consumer-"vibration" framing is pseudoscience — unfalsifiable, no
  mechanism, no demarcation.

## THE GENUINELY UNSOLVED CORE (what's actually open)
1. Is neural oscillatory coherence **causal** for inter-areal routing, or an epiphenomenal **consequence**
   of communication? (CTC vs coherence-through-communication.)
2. Is oscillation **necessary** for cognition, or one efficient implementation among others?
   (Transformers vs SSMs — a *direct, testable computational* question.)
3. Are EM-field identity theories (CEMI, Pockett) **falsifiable in practice**? Has the synchrony-vs-rate
   experiment actually been run decisively?
4. What *precisely* separates the legitimate core ("frequency is a universal descriptive language + a real
   neural mechanism") from the unfalsifiable leap ("frequency **is** the substrate of mind / essence of
   reality")? The panpsychist GRT in particular needs an explicit falsification criterion to be science.

**Honest bottom line:** vibration/frequency is a genuinely *universal mathematical language* for physics
and a *real mechanism* in neural dynamics. The strong claim — that it *is* the substrate of mind or the
*essence* of reality — is **unproven hypothesis at best, unfalsifiable metaphysics at worst.**
