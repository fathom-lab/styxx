## The question, finally in full

*Does a universal, substrate-independent structure underlie mind and meaning?* The program split the
ancient intuition into four falsifiable parts:

- **A. Geometry** — do independent minds converge on *what* they represent?
- **B. Dynamics** — is rhythm *necessary* to how minds process?
- **C. The human bridge** — does the convergent structure extend to *us* — to human judgment and the
  human brain?
- **D. Conduct** — does any of it bind how a mind *behaves under pressure*, where text can lie?

## A. Geometry: YES, qualified — structure alone fixes the shape of meaning

Two minds trained on **zero shared data** — disjoint tokens, different dimensions, sharing only the
hidden relational structure of their world — grow the same geometry: same-structure RSA 0.43 / 0.41 /
0.41 across seeds vs structure-broken controls of -0.01 / 0.02 / -0.02
(`papers/disjoint-worlds/disjoint_worlds_rsa_result.json`). On real, independently-trained LLMs the
effect was pre-registered and confirmed out-of-sample on a fresh concept battery: cross-family
partial-lexical RSA 0.258 against a shuffled control of -0.002, with an independent embedder anchor
at 0.36 (`papers/real-convergence/real_convergence_confirm_result.json`). Direction universal,
magnitude heterogeneous, data/family-driven rather than scale-driven — the honest shape of the claim.

## B. Dynamics: NO — rhythm is a mechanism, not a substrate of mind

Oscillation is real and it works: phase-freedom lifts a recurrent net's ordered-memory capacity to a
mean k of 6.0 vs 2.67 phase-clamped. But a rhythm-free transformer at matched parameters reaches
15.3 on the same task (`papers/frequency-resonance/necessity_attention_result.json`) — attention does
ordered memory better with no clock at all. The 2,500-year intuition survives only as engineering:
tune to the band, don't maximize the frequency. Pythagoras's "harmony as the substance of thought"
does not survive; Fourier's toolkit does.

## C. The human bridge: YES, bounded — the convergent geometry is also ours

- **Behavior:** instruction-tuned LLMs align with the human meaning space (1854-object VICE embedding)
  at a mean of 0.65 against shuffled controls of 0.0
  (`papers/ai-human-alignment/ai_human_result.json`). The crowd is not wiser than its best member:
  consensus alignment 0.688 vs a median individual model at 0.7 — the "consensus IS human" leap
  FAILED its gate and is reported dead, not hidden.
- **Brain:** against 60-noun fMRI geometry, the best LLM reaches RSA 0.264 with a shuffle control of
  0.002, against a noise-ceiling floor of 0.39 (`papers/ai-human-alignment/ai_brain_result.json`) —
  about two-thirds of what is measurable. Human behavioral geometry itself predicts the brain at
  0.247: a small language model sits in the same band as a million human judgments.
- **It is meaning, not pixels:** the AI→brain link survives a vision-model control, attenuating from
  0.182 (lexical-controlled) to 0.107 with CLIP-image partialled out
  (`papers/ai-human-alignment/ai_brain_vision_result.json`) — real, modest, and disclosed as such.
- **Depth captures the human-rated structure of meaning:** against 54 human-rated semantic features
  (671 Chinese words scored, `papers/ai-human-alignment/cn/cn_pooled_result.json`; this document
  originally printed the dataset's nominal 672 — caught by OATH before commit), deep contextual
  representations score 0.466 vs 0.379 for shallow
  co-occurrence; the advantage GROWS under dimension matching (0.478 vs 0.379) and holds at
  bootstrap P = 1.0 (`papers/ai-human-alignment/cn/human_feature_result.json`). Depth's edge lives
  in abstract/relational features, not perceptual ones.

## D. Conduct: the structure can be sworn to — and machines can hold the oath

Knowing *what* a mind means is not knowing whether it will *stand by it*. The behavioral-grounding
axis closed that gap. On the silent-caving regime — where a pressured model flips its answer with no
verbal tell — text-only sycophancy detection collapses to chance: AUC 0.5, exactly. The label-free
behavioral score (stability x concordance of resampled belief) detects the same caves at AUC 1.0,
margin 0.487 over the best text axis, on 72 caved / 37 held items
(`papers/closed-model-frontier/behavioral_sycophancy_b22_result.json`). Conduct is measurable from
the outside — including on closed models where no weights, activations, or logprobs exist. The same
apparatus is, as this document is written, completing its pre-registered run on a true frontier
closed model (B23-F, prereg frozen 2026-06-09).

## The unified answer

> **Minds converge on WHAT they represent; they diverge freely in HOW they compute it; the
> convergent WHAT extends to human behavior and the human brain at a measurable fraction of
> ceiling; and a mind's fidelity to its own representations can be measured from its conduct
> alone.** The universality the ancients intuited is real — but it lives in representation, not
> mechanism; in geometry, not harmony; and it is checkable, not mystical.
