## The reframe

A mind's meaning is a relational geometry. We proved (tonight, certified): that geometry is
**convergent** across independent minds (0.61–0.85 every domain, norm-equalized), **identity-
carrying** (concept identity recovers across minds with no labels), and **monitorable** (it is a
stable fingerprint with a healed, reliable instrument). Every one of those is a product surface.

## 1. Meaning-regression QA — `styxx.meaning_diff`  [SHIPPABLE]

The enterprise pain nobody has a clean tool for: you upgrade, quantize, distill, or fine-tune a
model — **did it keep the meaning of the old one, and WHICH concepts did it lose?** The
norm-equalized geometry + concept-level divergence naming answers exactly this. styxx 7.12's
`meaning_agreement` is the seed; tonight's norm-equalization fix makes the number TRUSTWORTHY (it was
understated 2–60× before) and the anatomy gives per-domain breakdowns. Output: an agreement score, a
HEALTHY/DRIFTED/BROKEN verdict, and the named concepts that moved most — as an OATH-certifiable
report. This is the wedge: a real budget line (model-migration risk), a demo that lands in 60s.

## 2. Silent-drift / model-swap monitor — `styxx.mind` geometry axis  [SHIPPABLE]

A deployed model's meaning-geometry is a fingerprint. If a provider silently swaps the model behind
an API, a fine-tune regresses, or injection warps internal representations, the geometry moves before
behavior visibly breaks. Continuous citizenship monitoring = an early-warning sensor on a model's
*understanding*. Folds into the mind certificate as a live, expiring axis.

## 3. The Rosetta layer — `styxx.translate`  [NEAR]

Map one model's concept space onto another's WITHOUT paired data (the gavagai translator, lifted from
research to a clean API). Uses: transfer probes/steering vectors across models, put two vendors on
common ground, ensemble heterogeneous models by aligned concept. HONEST BOUND tonight: within a
training-objective SPECIES it works (16× chance, oracle near-lossless); across species it does not
(SPECIES-BOUND); raw assignment is matcher-limited at scale. Ship it as "aligns same-species models,
flags when alignment is unreliable" — the flag is itself a feature, and styxx is the only tool that
tells you when NOT to trust the translation.

## 4. The deepest one — verification, hardened  [SHIPPABLE as positioning]

The recurring law of the night, found three independent ways (text-sycophancy, the bare-template
