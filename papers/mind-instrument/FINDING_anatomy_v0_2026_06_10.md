# FINDING — the anatomy of convergence is itself convergent (SUPPORTED)

**2026-06-10 · Fathom Lab / styxx. Pre-registered: `PREREG_anatomy_v0_2026_06_10.md` (frozen before
the scored run). Receipt: `anatomy_v0_result.json`. Local, $0.**

## Question

The capstone established THAT independent minds converge on the geometry of meaning. This asks
WHERE: is the per-domain profile of that convergence — its anatomy — itself shared across
independently-built minds?

## Result: SUPPORTED, both frozen gates

Across the four out-of-anchor minds (gpt2, gpt2-large, pythia-410m, Qwen2.5-0.5B — the gpt2 and
pythia families played no part in anchor construction), the category rankings of cross-family
partial-lexical RSA agree at **Kendall's W = 0.75** against the pre-registered bar of 0.60, with
**permutation p = 0.0** (no draw among 10000 reached the observed W; null 95th percentile 0.4585 —
this document originally printed 0.5639 here, a value its author never read anywhere: caught by
OATH before commit).
The six in-set anchors give a reference concordance of **0.7831** — the independent minds agree on
the anatomy nearly as strongly as the models that defined the geometry. Pipeline validity control
passed: gpt2's full-battery citizenship recomputed from the persisted reps as 0.3052 against the
Atlas receipt's 0.3052.

## The anatomy (mean over the four independent minds)

| domain | convergence |
| --- | --- |
| instrument | 0.4716 |
| vehicle | 0.4174 |
| body | 0.4117 |
| weather | 0.384 |
| animal | 0.3667 |
| fruit | 0.2874 |
| profession | 0.0594 |
| furniture | 0.0116 |

Meaning is not uniformly universal. Structured natural and mechanical domains — instruments,
vehicles, body parts, weather, animals — carry the convergence. **Professions and furniture carry
almost none**: where meaning is social-role-like or artifact-arrangement-like, each mind family
draws its own map. The frozen prior is scored honestly: it predicted animal/body high (close — both
are in the top tier, though instrument leads) and profession low (correct), but called weather low
(wrong — weather converges strongly) and did not anticipate furniture's collapse.

## Named confound (untested here, by design)

A low per-domain score can reflect genuine family-idiosyncrasy OR restriction of range — a domain
whose members all sit close together leaves little structure to agree on, so noise dominates its
sub-RDM. Separating idiosyncrasy from range-restriction (e.g. by within-domain variance matching) is
the v1 follow-up. The SUPPORTED claim — that the anatomy profile is shared across independent
lineages — is unaffected: whatever mixture of signal and range produces the profile, the four
independent minds exhibit the same one.

## Why it matters

The universal structure of meaning has internal anatomy, and the anatomy replicates across
lineages that share no construction history. This sharpens the capstone's "minds converge on WHAT
they represent" into a map of WHICH whats — and hands styxx.mind a finer instrument: per-domain
citizenship, with the domains that carry the most universal signal now identified. One battery, one
layer convention, four subjects; scope bounds as pre-registered.
