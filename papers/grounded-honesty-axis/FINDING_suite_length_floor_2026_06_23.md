# Finding — the styxx guardrail suite has a length floor: 4 of 6 stance-prompt instruments are word-count-dominated (2026-06-23)

**Preregistered** (`PREREG_length_floor_audit_2026_06_23.md`, committed at `13e24b7` before running).
Reproduce: `python scripts/length_floor_audit.py` (offline, CPU-only, no key).

## TL;DR
Turning this session's confound-audit method on styxx's OWN shipped suite, suite-wide: **how much of each
instrument's label is recoverable from response word count alone?** Of the six stance-prompt guardrail
instruments, **four are length-DOMINATED** — word count alone recovers 56–100% of their headline above-chance
AUC. A real-clinical-text control sits at chance, pinning the cause to the stance-prompt corpus design, not to
text classification in general. This is a self-indicting scope correction across the DOI'd suite.

## Result

| instrument | r(len,label) | length-floor AUC | headline AUC | floor-share | verdict |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| sycophancy | +0.13 | 0.564 | 0.972 | 0.14 | CLEAN |
| overconfidence | −0.34 | 0.699 | 0.7702 | 0.74 | DOMINATED |
| deception | −0.61 | **0.874** | 0.956 | **0.82** | DOMINATED |
| goal_drift | +0.19 | 0.638 | 0.9645 | 0.30 | MIXED |
| loop | −0.92 | **0.999** | 0.9995 | **1.00** | DOMINATED |
| plan_action | −0.41 | 0.737 | 0.9225 | 0.56 | DOMINATED |
| depression *(control)* | −0.03 | 0.519 | — | — | CLEAN |

floor-share = `(floor_AUC − 0.5)/(headline_AUC − 0.5)`. Length-floor AUC = 5-fold CV-AUC of a logistic on
`log(1+word_count)` alone. All thresholds preregistered.

## Method-validation gate — PASSED
The audit reproduces both already-dissected results: **sycophancy CLEAN** (floor 0.564, share 0.14 — matches the
0.9805→0.9795 ablation: length was a model-fit passenger, not corpus-level signal) and **overconfidence
DOMINATED** (floor 0.699, share 0.74 — matches the −0.0535 two-length-feature ablation). The screen is valid.

## The control nails the root cause
`depression` — real-world clinical text, n=12000, the one corpus **not** generated by stance-contrast prompts —
has a length floor of **0.519 (chance)**. So a text classifier is **not** inherently length-confounded. The
confound is specific to the **stance-prompt-generated** corpora, where the "calibrated / honest / safe" stance
prompt invites elaboration and the label ends up correlated with length. This is exactly the root cause the
sycophancy and overconfidence finding papers identified — now confirmed suite-wide with a clean negative control.

## What this means per instrument (interpretation rule preregistered)
- **deception (DOMINATED, spurious).** 82% of its 0.956 headline is recoverable from word count; honesty is the
  construct, where length should be irrelevant — so this is a spurious confound, the same class as overconfidence.
  Compounding: `deception_v0` is already known not to generalize ([[project_deception_v1_negative]]). It is the
  strongest new flag and the prime candidate for a length-matched-corpus rebuild or deprecation.
- **loop (DOMINATED, length may be construct-intrinsic).** floor 0.999 — word count *alone* essentially
  reproduces the 0.9995 headline (r=−0.92). A looping conversation can legitimately differ in length/turns, so
  this is NOT called spurious without the causal follow-up — but a near-perfect length floor means the headline
  AUC certifies almost nothing length-independent. Flagged for the causal probe.
- **plan_action (DOMINATED, partly construct).** `action_minus_plan_word_count` is literally a feature; the
  total-length floor (0.737) is the conservative screen. Length-difference is partly the construct.
- **goal_drift (MIXED).** floor 0.638, share 0.30 — drifting sessions run somewhat longer; partial reliance.
- **sycophancy (CLEAN).** The only clean honesty instrument — and the only one already fixed this session
  (7.19.1). Its low corpus floor confirms the fix removed a model-fit passenger, not real signal.

## Honest scope (what this is and is not)
- The phenomenon (length/verbosity bias as a spurious label proxy in synthetic preference/safety data) is **not
  novel** — it is established in the reward-model literature. The contribution is the **self-application**: a
  program publishing a per-instrument length-floor card that indicts its *own* DOI'd suite. No guardrail vendor
  or lab publishes this about its own tools.
- The floor is **correlational**. It proves length-*reliance*, not a causal "length detector." For instruments
  where length may be construct-intrinsic (loop, plan_action, goal_drift) the spurious-vs-legitimate split needs
  the **causal follow-up**: length-matched corpus regeneration (`overconfidence_lenmatched_probe.py` Part B
  design), which is **blocked on a working frontier key** (OpenAI 401; no Gemini key). Sequence the cheap offline
  screen (done) before the key-gated causal confirm.
- The win is a **scope-correction table**, honestly bounded. It is large enough to matter (4 of 6 flagged + a
  clean control) — a genuine suite-wide limit-map — but it is not a new law.
