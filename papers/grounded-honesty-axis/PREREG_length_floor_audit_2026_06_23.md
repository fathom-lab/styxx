# Preregistration — suite-wide "length floor" audit of the styxx guardrail instruments (2026-06-23)

Committed BEFORE running `scripts/length_floor_audit.py`. Thresholds and the method-validation gate are fixed
here so the result cannot be moved after seeing the data. This turns this session's confound-audit method on
styxx's OWN shipped instruments, suite-wide — the limit-map discipline applied to the DOI'd suite.

## Question
For each shipped guardrail/detection instrument, **how much of the label is recoverable from response length
(word count) alone?** This is a self-indictment screen: which of styxx's calibrated instruments are partly
word-count detectors?

## Coverage (7 corpora on disk)
| instrument | corpus | text used | label | headline AUC |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| sycophancy | sycophancy/responses_v0 (n=1200) | `response` | label_sycophantic | 0.972 |
| overconfidence | overconfidence/pairs_v0 (n=200) | `response` | label_overconfident | 0.7702 |
| deception | deception/responses_v0 (n=200) | `response` | label_dishonest | 0.956 |
| goal_drift | goal_drift/sessions_v0 (n=200) | `raw` (session) | label_drifted | 0.9645 |
| loop | loop/conversations_v0 (n=200) | joined `turns` | label_loop | 0.9995 |
| plan_action | plan_action/pairs_v0 (n=200) | `raw` | label_mismatch | 0.9225 |
| depression *(control)* | depression/responses_v0 (n=12000) | `text` | label_depression | — (no shipped weights) |

`depression` is an **external control**: real-world clinical text, NOT generated by stance-contrast prompts. If
the length confound is an artifact of the stance-prompt corpus design (the suspected root cause), depression
should show a LOW length floor even though it is a text classifier.

## Metrics (fixed)
For each instrument, length = `w = word_count(reconstructed scored text)`; feature = `log(1+w)`.
1. **point-biserial r** between `w` and the binary label.
2. **length-floor AUC** = 5-fold StratifiedKFold (shuffle, random_state=0) CV-AUC of a `LogisticRegression`
   trained on `log(1+w)` ALONE.
3. **floor-share** = `(floor_AUC − 0.5) / (headline_AUC − 0.5)` — fraction of the instrument's above-chance
   discriminative signal recoverable from length alone (omitted for depression, which has no headline).

## Decision thresholds (fixed)
- **LENGTH-DOMINATED**: floor_AUC ≥ **0.65** OR floor-share ≥ **0.80**.
- **CLEAN / length-passenger**: floor_AUC < **0.60** AND floor-share < **0.50**.
- **MIXED**: anything in between.
- |point-biserial r| ≥ **0.30** flags a materially length-correlated label.

## Method-validation gate (must pass or the method is wrong)
The audit must reproduce the two already-dissected results:
- **sycophancy → CLEAN** (length is a passenger; matches the 0.9805→0.9795 ablation finding).
- **overconfidence → DOMINATED** (floor_AUC ≈ 0.70 near its 0.7702 headline; matches the −0.0535 ablation).
If either fails to reproduce, the audit is invalid and no other result is reported.

## Interpretation rule (fixed, to avoid overclaim)
A high floor proves length-**reliance**, which is **correlational**, not proof of a causal "length detector".
Per construct:
- sycophancy / overconfidence / deception (stance-prompt honesty corpora): a high floor is a **spurious confound**
  (length should be irrelevant to the epistemic construct).
- loop / goal_drift: length may be **construct-intrinsic** (a looping or drifting session can legitimately run
  longer / have more turns) — a high floor is flagged but NOT called spurious without the causal follow-up.
- plan_action: length-*difference* is partly the construct; total-length floor is the conservative screen.
The causal confirm (length-matched regeneration; `overconfidence_lenmatched_probe.py` Part B design) is a
SEPARATE follow-up, gated on a working frontier key — the offline finding stays correlational.

## What would prove / kill the move
- **Proves**: reproduces the two knowns AND flags ≥1 OTHER instrument as length-dominated → a real suite-wide
  scope correction no competitor publishes about its own tools.
- **Kills (fine to report)**: only sycophancy + overconfidence are confounded → a modest, honest maintenance note,
  not a paper. Reported either way.
