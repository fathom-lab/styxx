passage inside the governance charter reads as crypto-affiliation in exactly the rooms the
scorecard/G-series work is aimed at (EU AI Office orbit, grant committees). "Disqualifying in
Brussels; poisons grants." The network-coordination idea is not being deleted — it is being
QUARANTINED out of the governance charter into its own clearly-labeled experimental doc.

validator-tier coordination idea verbatim, headed "experimental; not part of the governance
charter; see OPEN_CORE.md for the binding rails."
