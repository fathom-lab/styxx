## Send-time tips

- Send to **2-3 clinics** in parallel (most have 4-6 week response times).
- Attach the **Spec v1.0 PDF** (downloadable from `fathom.darkflobi.com/cognometric-spec-v1.0.md` and convertible to PDF) so the recipient has a self-contained reference.
- Mention the **specific deadline date** in the subject line — clinic intake teams triage by urgency.
- If you receive **no response within 2 weeks**, re-send with subject prefix "[FOLLOW-UP]" — clinic emails get buried during admissions cycles.
